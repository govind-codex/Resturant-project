Our Story

More Than a Meal —
It's an Occasion

Nestled in the heart of Kanchan Bagh, Indore, Oakaz was born from a simple belief: every gathering deserves to feel extraordinary. Whether it's a quiet dinner for two or a celebration with fifty, we craft experiences that linger long after the last bite.

From our premium paneer creations to decadent desserts, from live music evenings to intimate private dining — every detail at Oakaz is curated with intentional warmth.
